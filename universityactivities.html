JSPM

Jawatankuasa Sekreteriat Parlimen Mahasiswa

  • It is a body formed at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) that serves as a platform for students to voice their opinions, concerns, and suggestions on campus issues. It functions like a parliamentary system, where its members, who are students, are allowed to play a role in making decisions on matters related to student welfare, campus development, and social or academic issues.
    In this club, I serve as the Exco Unit Penyata Rasmi.

Majlis Parlimen Mahasiswa

JSP

Jawatankuasa Sukan Pelajar

  • A body established at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) to manage and promote sports activities among students, the Jawatankuasa Sukan Pelajar (JSP) is responsible for organizing and coordinating various sports events and recreational activities to support the physical and mental development of students.
    In this club, I serve as the Sekretariat Multimedia Kreatif.
